Brownie

Description

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine dry ingredients in one bowl
  3. Combine wet ingredients in another bowl.
  4. Slowly mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  5. Mix until smooth.
  6. Pour into a greased pan.
  7. Bake for 35 minutes.
  8. Take out of oven and allow to cool.
